Welcome to AISafe App ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive information. All operations and data remain entirely on your device.

2. Works 100% Offline

AISafe App is designed to function entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection for any of its core functionalities. No data is sent to any server or third party.

3. App Lock & Fingerprint Authentication

We use Android’s BiometricPrompt API for fingerprint authentication. Fingerprint data is never accessed or stored by the app. It remains securely managed by your device’s operating system.

4. Accessibility Services

We request the Accessibility Service permission only to monitor the foreground app activity and lock selected apps. This data is never logged or shared, and is used solely within the device.

5. Overlay Permissions

We may request the “Draw over other apps” permission only to show the lock screen. No information is captured or transmitted.

6. Photo Locking and Encryption

Photos added to the locked folder are encrypted and stored locally. We use secure encryption methods such as AES or Android’s EncryptedFile API. No backups or remote storage is used.

7. AI Face Recognition (if enabled)

Face recognition is done on-device only. Facial data is not sent to servers. It is used solely to group or lock photos locally on your device.

8. No Ads or Third-Party SDKs

This app contains no ads and no analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.

9. Local Storage Only

All app data is stored only on your device. We do not use any cloud or remote storage.

10. User Responsibility

If you uninstall the app without backing up encrypted files, you may lose access. We cannot recover lost data.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Changes will be noted by updating the “Effective Date.”
